What is Glad Aura?

Glad Aura is India’s first life skills audio program where engaging stories become lasting habits. In one short, joyful weekly session, children listen, reflect, and practice — while parents enjoy a safe, ad-free experience they can trust.

🎯 Stories that teach

Two skill-based episodes + one calming bedtime story every week. Each one makes values and thinking skills easy to grasp.

⚡ Effortless learning

Only 20–30 minutes a week. No tests, no pressure — just natural growth through curiosity.

🌱 Skills for the real world

Focus, empathy, decision-making, emotional balance, digital discipline — woven into relatable, memorable stories.
